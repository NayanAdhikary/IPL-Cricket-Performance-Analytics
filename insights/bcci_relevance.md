# BCCI Performance Analyst Relevance

## Matches Job Description
- **Data Aggregation**: Merged 100k+ ball-by-ball records with match info [pandas merge]
- **Match Analytics**: Player KPIs (SR 145+, Econ 7.2), team strategies
- **Performance Insights**: Venue/toss impact, win predictors
- **Tech Stack**: Python/pandas, visualizations (Matplotlib/Seaborn)
- **Outputs**: CSVs, dashboards for coaching/sports science teams

**Skills Demonstrated**: Data cleaning, KPI computation, strategic reporting for player development pathways at BCCI CoE.**
